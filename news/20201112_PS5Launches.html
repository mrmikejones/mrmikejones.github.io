PS5 Launches

A collection of cool things from the launch of PS5.

Kyojin Myojin shrine

Kyojin Myojin shrine was lit up for the PS5 launch.

The site's proximity to Akihabara means it is today popular among tech geeks, and talismans are sold to bless electronic devices.
