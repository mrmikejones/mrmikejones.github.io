Champagne Loaf Starter

These are the quantities adjusted for 1 loaf. The original recipe is from Flour, Water, Salt, Yeast by Ken Forkish. The recipe quantities give excess starter to use for the next loaf. I have a starter that I keep, so it ends up in the bin which feels pretty wasteful.

  • 18g levain
  • 72g white flour
  • 18g whole wheat flour
  • 72g water

  • Flour, Water, Salt, Yeast by Ken Forkish
