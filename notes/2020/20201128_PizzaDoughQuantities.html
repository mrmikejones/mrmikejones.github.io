Pizza Dough Quantities

These are the quantities adjusted for 1 pizza. The original recipe is from Flour, Water, Salt, Yeast by Ken Forkish.

  • 140ml water
  • 1/8 tsp yeast
  • 200g flour
  • 4g salt
